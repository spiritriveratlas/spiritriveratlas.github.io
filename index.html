why we invest

We face many challenges, climate change, global healthcare events and rising inequality. We believe people are capable of creating a better world. We also believe that both science and technology will be instrumental to achieve that goal. Therefore, we invest in and help fundamental science and technology companies that have the potential to set a new standard or replace and improve on current processes. To be able to achieve this lofty target we need to dare and tackle the hard problems.
 

How we invest in you

We look for promising pre-seed and pre-revenue ventures to help them get started with both a ﬁnancial investment and hands-on help. We help with the initial push to Series A and are well placed to further be of use afterwards as advisors until Series B. We, Spirit River Atlas, are experienced operators. We’ve scaled teams in multi-billion scale-ups, brought nascent product ideas to investable maturity, founded startups and experienced and survived all the growing pains that comes with that. We invest a small amount to have skin in the game support the team to get to their next funding round.
